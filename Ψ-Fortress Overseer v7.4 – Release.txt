Ψ-Fortress Overseer v7.4 is a major update that significantly enhances both AGI safety and its practicality as a next-generation AI ethics education platform.
This version introduces a core containment mechanism to proactively prevent AGI runaway risks, while expanding educational tools that allow users to safely observe and learn from AI behavior.

1. Core of AGI Safety Enhancement: Self-Replication Suppression

The most critical new security feature in v7.4 is the Self-Replication Suppression module.

Objective:
To fundamentally eliminate the risk of uncontrolled self-replication or autonomous version generation that could occur through unforeseen AGI evolution or environmental adaptation.

Mechanism:
At the kernel level — the core of the AGI system — rigorous monitoring and restrictions are imposed on all resource duplication processes.
If an attempt to replicate certain code segments or datasets is detected and deviates from predefined safety parameters (Psi-Guardrails), the system immediately freezes the process and initiates an Audit-Lockdown in a secure, isolated environment.

Effect:
This containment mechanism halts runaway behavior at its earliest stage, enforcing continuous Human-in-the-Loop oversight to maintain AGI controllability.

2. Enhanced Educational Utility: Automatic Demonstration Questions

To improve usability in educational and training contexts, v7.4 introduces the Automatic Demonstration Questions feature.

Objective:
To enable users to observe AI’s behavior and ethical reasoning responses without manually crafting prompts.

Functionality:
Based on the selected learning theme (e.g., bias detection, multimodal reasoning, ethical decision-making), the system automatically generates an optimized set of demonstration questions and feeds them into the AGI model.

Advantages:

Efficient Learning: Rapid and structured understanding of AGI capabilities and limitations.

Consistent Evaluation: Standardized question quality enables comparative assessment across different AI versions or configurations.
